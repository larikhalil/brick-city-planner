Guide

What the buttons do — top bar

Save asSave this city under a name so you can reopen it later.
My citiesOpen, rename or delete the cities you've saved.
ImportLoad a city from a file on your device.
ShareCopy a link that reopens this exact city.
ExportDownload the city — as data, a picture, or a shopping list.
New cityStart over with an empty table.
TemplatesBegin from a ready-made layout instead of a blank table.
Kid ModeFreeze the layout so pieces can be added but not moved.
ThemeSwitch between light and dark.
Colourblind-safeAdd patterns so categories are distinct without relying on colour.
Scale referenceOverlay a minifig, car and ruler at true stud scale.
Magnetic snapSnaps pieces edge-to-edge. Hold Alt while dragging to place on any stud.

What the buttons do — canvas toolbar

Undo / Redo / HistoryStep back and forward, or jump to any point in your edits.
RotateTurn the selection 90° (or drag the handle above it for any angle).
Bring forward / Send backChange which pieces sit on top.
DuplicateMake a copy of the selection (also Ctrl+D).
DeleteRemove the selected pieces.
LockFreeze the selection so it can't be moved by accident.
LayersShow, hide or lock whole layers (ground, roads, buildings…).
ToolsSelect & move · paint terrain · drop a sticky note · draw a custom block.
TableSet how many baseplates wide × tall your table is.
ZoomZoom in or out, or Fit the whole city on screen.
Check my cityScan for buildability problems (off-plate, overlaps, track gaps…).
3D previewA read-only isometric view — drag to rotate.
Export imageSave a crisp PNG picture of your plan.
