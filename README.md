# LEGO City Planner

A digital, top-down **LEGO city planner** — design your LEGO city layout in a virtual space *before* buying the physical sets.

Browse a catalog of real LEGO sets, drag their footprints onto a grid that represents your table/floor, and see how the whole city fits together (total size, baseplate tiling, roads, budget).
